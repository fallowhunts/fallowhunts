About Fallow Hunts

Irish-Guided Fallow & Red Deer Hunting Adventures

Fallow Hunts is a family-run operation based in Munster, Ireland, led by father and son duo Michael and James Stapleton. With decades of experience in ethical deer management, we specialize in guided hunts for fallow and red deer across some of Ireland’s most breathtaking landscapes. Our mission is to offer authentic, safe, and unforgettable hunting experiences for visiting hunters—especially from the U.S.—who want to connect with Ireland’s rich tradition of deer stalking.

  • One-on-one guided stalking of fallow and red deer
  • Training in ethical hunting and firearm safety
  • Field dressing, carcass prep, and chilling facilities
  • Diverse terrain including mountains, forests, and lowlands

Additional Harvest Charges

The following additional fees apply if specific deer are harvested during your hunt:

  • Red Stag €300
  • Red Hind €200
  • Red Calf €100
  • Fallow Buck €200
  • Fallow Doe €100
  • Fallow Calf €50

Rifle and ammunition rental available if needed.

Note: Any wounded animal is considered a harvested animal and will be charged accordingly.
