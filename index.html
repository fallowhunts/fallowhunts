About MWD Hunting

Family-Run Wild Deer Management in Ireland

MWD Hunting is a father and son team, founded by Michael and James Stapleton, dedicated to the ethical and sustainable management of Ireland’s wild deer population. With decades of combined experience in deer stalking, we aim to share our knowledge, promote responsible hunting, and provide unforgettable guided hunting experiences for both beginners and seasoned stalkers.

  • One-to-one guided deer stalking sessions
  • Training in firearm safety and humane dispatch
  • Field dressing, carcass handling, and chilling process
  • Hunting in diverse terrain: mountains, woodland, and lowlands
